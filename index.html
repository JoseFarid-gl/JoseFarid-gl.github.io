About

I'm a certified IT delivery professional and servant leader who has spent the last decade at the intersection of Agile execution and enterprise governance — facilitating the flow of work for Scrum teams, coordinating cross-functional programs, and managing releases through CAB approvals in highly regulated environments.

My work spans bank divestitures, fraud prevention platforms, regulatory reporting automation, and large-scale system migrations. I coach teams through Agile maturity using Scrum, Kanban and SAFe, facilitate every Scrum event, and support other teams across the SAFe Agile Release Train (ART). I rely on information radiators — metrics and dashboards — to give teams visibility, remove bottlenecks and impediments early, and help them make better decisions that lead to better results. Strong communication and problem-solving let me engage everyone from developers to executive sponsors, and I promote continuous improvement in every sprint.

I'm currently pursuing a Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Colorado Boulder, and hold AI-Empowered SAFe 6 Scrum Master certification — combining proven delivery discipline with an AI-forward toolkit, including Anthropic's Claude Code training for project managers.
